# Hospital Boulders

> **Tier**: Supplementary
> **Drive from lodging**: ~1.5–2 hrs from Enterprise South Nature Park area [UNVERIFIED]

## Overview

Hospital Boulders is an easy-access bouldering area north of Gadsden, AL, on the west brow of Lookout Mountain. With 200+ sandstone problems and an approach measured in hundreds of feet from parking, it's one of the most convenient areas in the region. Owned and managed by the Southeastern Climbers Coalition, it's a solid moderate-climbing destination that happens to be along the corridor toward HP40.

- **Drive time**: ~1.5–2 hrs from Enterprise South Nature Park area [UNVERIFIED] — along the I-59 corridor toward HP40
- **Parking**: Near Mountain View Hospital off I-59
- **Approach**: Very short — few hundred feet from parking to boulders

## Access

- **Ownership**: SCC-owned (purchased 2012)
- **Fees**: Free, but requires gate code
- **Permits**: Fill out form on SCC website to receive gate code
- **Closures**: Dawn to dusk only. No overnight camping. Dogs on leash OK.
- **Etiquette**: **SCC does not own the entire boulder field** — watch for "End SCC Property" signs. Climbing beyond them is trespassing.
