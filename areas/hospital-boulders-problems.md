# Hospital Boulders: Problem Clusters

> **Area Profile**: [Hospital Boulders](hospital-boulders.md)
> Easy-access SCC-owned area near the HP40 corridor with strong moderate climbing and abundant easy options. Short approach from parking makes it ideal for a low-friction session.

## V7–V10 (Hard)

> **Density**: Limited — only 3 confirmed hard problems from web sources. Hard data above V7 is thin [UNVERIFIED]. Additional hard problems may exist in the Kill Taker area — consult KAYA guidebook.

### Eddie Area (~5 min from parking)

- **Eddie** (V7) — Starts at back of cave, climbs hanging block to slopey topout. (B1 in SCC topo.) [MP, RV Project]
- **Hustle and Flow** (V10) — Sit start on pinch with toe hook; area's signature hard problem. (K6 in SCC topo.) [MP, Betacache, 27crags, KAYA]

### Barn Area (~5 min from parking)

- **Smash Alley** (V8) — Sit start on blunt tufa arete; traverse lip to seam. [MP, KAYA]

---

## V4–V6 (Moderate)

> **Density**: Strong — this is the area's sweet spot. Eight quality moderate problems spread across multiple sub-areas, all within a short walk of parking. Madness and Mystified in the Eddie corridor are near the V7–V10 hard climbing (Eddie V7, Hustle and Flow V10), so mixed-ability groups can share the same zone.

### Eddie Area (~5 min from parking)

- **Madness** (V5) — Begins the Eddie corridor; area classic. [MP, theCrag, KAYA]
- **Mystified** (V5) — Area classic. [MP]

### Barn Area (~5 min from parking)

- **Low & Slow** (V4) — [KAYA]
- **Aggravated Assault I** (~V5) — [MP, 27crags]

### Middle Area (~5–8 min from parking)

- **Get Low** (V4) — [KAYA]
- **Savage Problem** (V5) — [KAYA]

### Lower Area (~5–8 min from parking)

- **Angina** (V5) — [KAYA]
- **Deadhead** (V6) — [MP, Betacache]

## V0–V3 (Easy)

> **Density**: Abundant — Hospital Boulders has the best easy climbing of any Tier 2 area. Seven named problems across multiple sub-areas, all with short approaches. Ideal warmup or rest-day destination.
