# Cumberland Boulders (Pep Boys): Problem Clusters

> **Area Profile**: [Cumberland Boulders / Pep Boys](cumberland-pep-boys.md)
> Roof-heavy area on Mowbray Mountain (~5–10 min from Stone Fort). ~57 problems. Known for the 7-11 Roof and Hueco Roof. Quick-drying after rain.

## V7–V10 (Hard)

> **Density**: Strong — concentrated on signature roof features. This is the area's primary draw.

### 7-11 Roof (~15–20 min from parking, across the river)

- **Big Gulp** (V7) — Roof variation. _Overhung/sheltered._ [MP]
- **Slurpee** (V8) — Roof variation. _Overhung/sheltered._ [MP]
- **7/11** (V10) — Signature massive roof. _Overhung/sheltered._ [MP]

### Raindance / Southern Slang Area (~15–20 min from parking)

- **War Ready** (V5) — Classic roof problem. Iconic Suck Creek problem [MP]
- **Raindance** (V8) — Steep, crimpy overhang. [MP]
- **Walls of Illusion** (V9) — Sit start up an arete. [MP]
- **Southern Slang** (V11) — Steep. [MP]

### Penthouse (~15 min from parking)

- **Penthouse** (V8–V9) — Pure roof lines. _Sloped landings — spotter essential._ [MP]

### Riverfront (~10–15 min from parking, across creek)

- **Damn Yankees** (V9) — [MP]
- **Orange Juice** (V9) — Pinches and compression to topout. [MP]

### Texas Toast (~20 min past Forty Five)

- **Texas Toast** (V9–V10) — Further hike. [MP]

### Gross's Roof

- **Gross's Roof** (V11–V13) — Elite-level roof. [MP]

## V4–V6 (Moderate)

> **Density**: Moderate — Forty Five Boulders are the heart of moderate climbing here.

### Forty Five Boulders (~12–15 min from parking)

- **Left 45** (V4) — Steep. [MP]

## V0–V3 (Easy)

> **Density**: Limited — ~5–8 named problems. The Hueco Roof cluster is the best zone for easier climbing. The Warm Up Boulder has additional unnamed V0–V3 options. Not a primary destination for easy grades.

### Warm Up Boulder (~10 min from parking)

- **Ear** (V2) — First boulders encountered on the trail. [MP]
- **Bounce Test** (~V0–V3) [UNVERIFIED grade] — Near No Dice. [MP]

### Hueco Roof (~10–15 min from parking)

- **Peanut Gallery** (V1) — Roof on jugs. _Creek-side landing._ [MP]
- **Finagler** (V2) — Roof. _Creek-side landing._ [MP]
- **Hueco Roof Right** (V3) — "Start on the big ledge on the right side of the base of the roof. Surf jugs and battle the top." 9 ft. _3.1 stars, 86 votes — most popular easy problem in the area._ [MP]
- **Hueco Roof Left** (V4) — Harder left variation. [MP]
- **Over the River** (V4) — [MP]

### Lower Suck / Paul Bunyan Area (~15 min from parking)

- **Hatchet** (V4) — "Start as you would for Paul Bunyan Traverse, or use a left hand undercling, and engineer big moves straight up." 7 ft. _Power + reach friendly._ [MP]
