# Walden's Ridge Park: Problem Clusters

> **Area Profile**: [Walden's Ridge Park](waldens-ridge.md)
> Closest bouldering to Chattanooga (~25–35 min). Opened 2023. Primarily a moderate-climbing destination with minimal hard options. Free, no permits, lowest-friction access of any area.

## V7–V10 (Hard)

> **Density**: Minimal — only 1 confirmed V7+ problem. Hard climbing is not this area's strength. See HP40, Rocktown, or Stone Fort for deeper V7–V10 options.

### Boy Scout Trail (~10–15 min from parking)

- **God's Gas** (V7) — Stand start: undercling/pinch to sloper compression, bump to lip, loaf rail feature, topout. [MP, KAYA]

---

## V4–V6 (Moderate)

> **Density**: Strong — this is the area's primary strength. Twelve quality moderate problems concentrated around the Upper Parking Area and Falls Connector, with the V4–V5 range particularly well-represented. _Forested; generally shaded._
