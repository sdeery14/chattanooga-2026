# Oak Mountain State Park

> **Location**: 200 Terrace Dr, Pelham, AL 35124 (~20 mi south of Birmingham)
> **Drive from lodging**: ~2.5 hrs from Enterprise South Nature Park area
> **Nearby climbing**: None within 30 min. HP40 is the closest at ~1-1.5 hrs [UNVERIFIED].

> [!NOTE]
> At ~2.5 hrs from ESNP and ~1-1.5 hrs from HP40, this is a major detour. Only viable if the group splits for a full day or on the drive to/from HP40. Included for completeness.

## Overview

Alabama's largest state park (9,940 acres) with ~50 miles of trail carrying an IMBA Epic Ride designation — one of only 52 worldwide. Terrain ranges from beginner-friendly lake loops around Double Oak Lake to expert rock gardens at Blood Rock, with ~1,600 ft of combined climbing on the main loop. A destination unto itself offering a full day of serious, diverse riding.

**Difficulty**: Green (beginner) through Black (expert) — IMBA Epic Ride

## Access & Logistics

- **Parking**: Park entrance at 200 Terrace Dr, Pelham, AL
- **Fees**: $5/person (age 12+), $2 (age 4-11), $2 (seniors 62+). Free for under 4, active/retired military, veterans. Annual pass: $180.
- **February status**: Open year-round (Alabama State Park)
- **Hours**: Alabama State Park hours
- **Conditions**: Most sections shed water well, but bottom connector gets muddy. Clay soil in places. Check [BUMP trail status](https://www.bump.org/trails/oakmountain/) before driving.
