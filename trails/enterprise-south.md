# Enterprise South Nature Park

> **Location**: 190 Still Hollow Loop, Chattanooga, TN 37416 (NE Chattanooga, off I-75)
> **Drive from lodging**: 0 min (at lodging)
> **Nearby climbing**: [Walden's Ridge](../areas/waldens-ridge.md) (~25-35 min), [Stone Fort](../areas/stone-fort.md) (~35-45 min)

## Overview

A 2,800-acre county park with ~15 miles of well-groomed singletrack ranging from smooth beginner flow to jump-heavy advanced lines. Excellent variety in a single location -- boardwalks, pump track, rhythm sections, and technical features -- making it the most convenient ride from our lodging.

## Trails & Difficulty

IMBA-rated Green through Black. Key loops:

| Trail | Rating | Distance | Notes |
|-------|--------|----------|-------|
| Black Forest | Green | ~3 mi | Smooth beginner-friendly flow |
| TNT | Blue | ~4.5 mi | Jumps, rhythm sections |
| Log Rhythm | Blue-Black | -- | Technical features, long log feature, pump track area |
| Atlas | Black | ~5.5 mi | Jumps, most advanced loop |
| Full Loop | Blue (overall) | ~16 mi | Links all loops into one ride |

A pump track area is also available near the trails.

## Conditions

- **Drainage**: Good. Trails lined with small rock that drains quickly with little mud. Drainage areas can still get muddy after heavy rain.
- **Freeze/thaw**: Moderate concern -- rock-lined trails help but are not immune.
- **February outlook**: Generally rideable if no recent heavy rain or freeze/thaw cycle.

## Access & Logistics

- **Parking**: Multiple parking areas throughout the 2,800-acre park
- **Fees**: Free (Hamilton County park)
- **February status**: Open year-round. **[UNVERIFIED]** Park may be closed Saturday 2/14/26 (per a Yelp listing -- call to confirm).
- **Hours**: ~7:00 AM to 5:30-6:30 PM in February (varies by sunset; call 423-209-5350 to confirm)
